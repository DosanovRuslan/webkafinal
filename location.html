ILLNESSES

Obesity

Reason: The high level of calories often found in fast food contributes to the accumulation of excess weight.

Effect: Obesity increases the risk of various medical problems, including heart disease, diabetes, arthritis and some types of cancer.

Diabetes

Reason: Regular consumption of high-calorie foods rich in sugars and saturated fats can lead to insulin resistance and the development of type 2 diabetes.

Effect: Type 2 diabetes increases the risk of cardiovascular disease, increases blood sugar levels and has negative effects on organs.

Cardiovascular diseases

Reason: High levels of saturated fats and sugars can lead to atherosclerosis, high blood pressure and other factors that contribute to cardiovascular disease.

Effect: The risk of myocardial infarction, stroke, and other serious problems with the heart and blood vessels increases.
